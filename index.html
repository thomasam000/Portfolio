About Me

I was born and raised in the small town of Manson, Washington. I was the third of four children. I lived in Manson through highschool but had plans of leaving my small town.

The summer after senior year I submitted my application to serve a two year mission for the church of Jesus Christ of Latter Days Saints. I then left that fall to New York City, my new home for the next two years of my life.

Following those two years I moved to Utah to begin my post secondary education. However, after one semester I felt lost. I soon decided to move back to New York to discover myself and figure out where I was headed.
